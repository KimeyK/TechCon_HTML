Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for developers and has grown into a global tech movement. It has hosted events across five continents, inspiring developers, designers, and entrepreneurs alike.

Our Mission

We aim to foster innovation, connect visionaries, and push the boundaries of technology. TechCon supports diversity, learning, and global collaboration in the digital age.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Zhang: AI expert who built real-time voice-to-text tools for accessibility.

Samir Patel: Cloud architect known for designing scalable data infrastructures.

Leila Moore: Cybersecurity specialist and key advocate for women in tech.
